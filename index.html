About Jasmine Tea

Our Jasmine tea comes from the reputable Fujian province of China. Jasmine tea first appeared in official records around the 13th century, however it was reserved only for royalty. This year we’re bringing you our freshest Jasmine green tea, so you too can drink like royalty!

How to Brew Jasmine Green Tea

1. Place 1-2 tsp of jasmine green tea in a traditional Gaiwan (chinese tea pot). If you’re using a larger tea pot, double the amount.

2. Heat water in a kettle to 175 - 195°F

3. Pour the water and let the tea steep for 1-3 minutes depending on how strong you’d like your tea.

4. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
